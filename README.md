General
- Works entirely over your local network - open the app on your PC, scan the QR code on your phone
- Installs as a home screen app on your phone (no app store required)
- Automatically detects which aircraft you're flying and loads the right control profile
- Updates itself automatically in the background - you'll always be on the latest version
- Automatically sets up the Windows firewall rule it needs, no manual configuration required

Autopilot (Fenix A320)
- Control speed, heading, altitude and vertical speed directly from your phone
- Dedicated push/pull buttons for managed vs selected mode on each FCU encoder
- Toggle between knots and mach, set barometer pressure, switch STD/QNH
- Switch between 100ft and 1000ft altitude increment modes
- AP1, AP2, autothrottle, LOC and approach buttons - all highlight green when active in the sim

Switches
- Gear up/down, flaps, parking brake
- Full external lighting: nav/logo, beacon, strobe, landing lights, nose light
- Autobrake LO, MED and MAX

Camera
- Drag anywhere on screen to move the cockpit camera
- Snap buttons to jump to preset camera angles (F5-F9)

Radio (requires vPilot)
- See your COM1 and COM2 frequencies, swap active/standby, dial in a new standby frequency
- Live list of ATC stations in range with their frequencies
- Full radio message feed - both radio and private messages
- Send radio calls or direct messages straight from your phone

Map
- Live moving map showing your aircraft position on OpenStreetMap
- Tap the recenter button to lock the map to follow your aircraft
- Flight trail that draws behind the plane as you fly - colour coded by altitude: orange at low level, purple around 30,000ft, dark blue at 40,000ft, red above 50,000ft
- Load your SimBrief flight plan to overlay your planned route with origin, destination and waypoints
